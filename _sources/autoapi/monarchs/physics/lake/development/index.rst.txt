monarchs.physics.lake.development
=================================

.. py:module:: monarchs.physics.lake.development

.. autoapi-nested-parse::

   Lake development physics.

   Contains functions for the thermodynamic evolution of a fully-formed lake
   (depth >= 10 cm), including turbulent mixing, radiative transfer through
   the water column, and adjustment of the lake-firn boundary.

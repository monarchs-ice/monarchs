monarchs.physics.lake.lake_formation
====================================

.. py:function:: monarchs.physics.lake.lake_formation(cell, dt, met_data)

   Generate a lake, and track its evolution until we reach the point where
   it can evolve freely according to lake_development, when it goes about
   10 cm deep.
   Called in timestep_loop.

   :Parameters: * **cell** (*numpy structured array*) -- Element of the model grid we are operating on.
                * **dt** (*int*) -- Number of seconds in the timestep, most likely 3600 (i.e. 1 hour) [s]
                * **met_data** (*numpy structured array*) -- Structured array containing meteorological data used in the model.

   :rtype: None (amends cell inplace).

monarchs.physics.lid.initialise_lid
===================================

.. py:function:: monarchs.physics.lid.initialise_lid(cell, met_data, dt, dz, k_lid)

   Check to determine if a lid has been formed previously - if not, then
   initialise the lid by calculating its surface energy balance

   :Parameters: * **cell** (*numpy structured array*) -- Element of the model grid we are operating on.
                * **Q** (*float*) -- Surface energy flux, taken from surface_fluxes.sfc_flux. [W m^-2]

   :rtype: None (amends cell inplace)

src.monarchs.physics.lateral_functions.TemporaryCell
====================================================

.. py:class:: src.monarchs.physics.lateral_functions.TemporaryCell(lake_depth, water, saturation, meltflag, lake)

   Define a temporary class for us to store info on where water has moved to and from.
   This allows us to do the lateral movement "simultaneously", rather than moving it iteratively
   (causing the end result to be determined by which cells you start moving water from)

   :ivar lake_depth: lake depth of the IceShelf we are mirroring
   :vartype lake_depth: float
   :ivar water: water content of the IceShelf
   :vartype water: array_like, float, dimension(cell.vert_grid)
   :ivar saturation: boolean array determining whether the IceShelf is saturated at each vertical point

   :vartype saturation: array_like, bool, dimension(cell.vert_grid)


   .. py:attribute:: lake


   .. py:attribute:: lake_depth


   .. py:attribute:: meltflag


   .. py:attribute:: saturation


   .. py:attribute:: water

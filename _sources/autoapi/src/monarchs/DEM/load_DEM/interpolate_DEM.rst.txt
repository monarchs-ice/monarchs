src.monarchs.DEM.load_DEM.interpolate_DEM
=========================================

.. py:function:: src.monarchs.DEM.load_DEM.interpolate_DEM(heights, num_points)

   :Parameters: * **heights** (*array_like, float, dimension(lat, long)*) -- Array of heights from the DEM, creating a heightmap of the area we want to interpolate to our model space.
                * **num_points** (*int*) -- Number of points we want to interpolate the DEM onto. This currently just works for square grids, i.e.
                  row_amount == col_amount.

   :rtype: interp((X, Y)) - DEM interpolated onto the regular grid defined by num_points.

monarchs.core.initial_conditions.rho_init_emp

monarchs.core.initial_conditions.rho_init_emp(z, rho_sfc, z_t)

Initialise the firn column with a density derived from an empirical formula. This follows Paterson, W. (2000). The Physics of Glaciers. Butterworth-Heinemann, using the formula of Schytt, V. (1958). Glaciology. A: Snow studies at Maudheim. Glaciology. B: Snow studies inland. Glaciology. C: The inner structure of the ice shelf at Maudheim as shown by core drilling. Norwegian-British- Swedish Antarctic Expedition, 1949-5, IV.)

Parameters:

  • z (float)

  • rho_sfc (float) – Density that you desire for the surface firn layer. [kg m^-3]

  • z_t (float)

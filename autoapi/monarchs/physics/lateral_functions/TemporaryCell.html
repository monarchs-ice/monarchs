monarchs.physics.lateral_functions.TemporaryCell

class monarchs.physics.lateral_functions.TemporaryCell(lake_depth, water, saturation, meltflag)

Define a temporary class for us to store info on where water has moved to and from. This allows us to do the lateral movement “simultaneously”, rather than moving it iteratively (causing the end result to be determined by which cells you start moving water from)

Variables:

  • lake_depth (float) – lake depth of the IceShelf we are mirroring

  • water (array_like, float, dimension(cell.vert_grid)) – water content of the IceShelf

  • saturation (array_like, bool, dimension(cell.vert_grid)) – boolean array determining whether the IceShelf is saturated at each vertical point
